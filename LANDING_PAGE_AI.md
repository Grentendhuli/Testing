## ❓ FAQ (AI-First Framing)

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**
> A: ChatGPT doesn't know your tenants, your leases, or NYC housing law. LandlordBot is trained on your portfolio and NYC regulations. It doesn't just answer questions — it takes action.

**Q: What if the AI gets something wrong?**
> A: The AI suggests, you decide. Think of it as an extremely capable intern. For anything over $200 or legally sensitive, it asks for your approval.

**Q: Do I need to learn how to use it?**
> A: No. If you can send a text message, you can use LandlordBot. Just type what you want like you're talking to a person.

**Q: How does this compare to a human property manager?**
> A: Humans charge $1,000-3,000/month and work business hours. LandlordBot is $75/month and never sleeps. It's not a replacement for your judgment — it's the assistant you wish you had.

**Q: Is my data safe with AI?**
> A: Yes. We don't use your data to train external AI models. All processing is encrypted, and AI runs on isolated instances. Your tenant data stays private.

**Q: Can I try it before paying?**
> A: Absolutely. The free tier lets you manage up to 3 properties with full AI access. Upgrade only when you're ready to scale.
