## The Hidden Time Drain of Traditional Property Management

Let's look at where your time actually goes as a landlord:

### Manual Tenant Screening Takes 3-4 Hours Per Application

Every time a prospective tenant applies, you're manually:
- Reviewing credit reports and deciphering scores
- Calling previous landlords for references  
- Verifying employment and income documentation
- Evaluating background checks
- Making go/no-go decisions

That's 3-4 hours **per application**. With typical vacancy periods requiring 10-15 showings, you're looking at 30-60 hours just to fill one unit.

### Rent Collection Creates 40+ Monthly Emails

Follow-ups. Reminders. "Did you get my check?" questions. Payment confirmations. Late notices. Escalations.

If you manage 10 units and each tenant needs 4 touchpoints per month, that's 40+ emails and messages you're writing, sending, and tracking. Every single month.

### Compliance Tracking Requires Constant Vigilance

HPD registrations. FDNY inspections. DOB permits. Lead paint certifications. Smoke detector checks. Heat season requirements. Façade inspections.

NYC has more compliance requirements than most cities, andmissing a deadline can cost you $250-$1,000+ in fines.

The result? You're constantly checking calendars, setting reminders, and stressing about what you might have missed.

**The total time cost:** 10+ hours per week on administrative tasks instead of growing your portfolio or enjoying your life.
